What is SkyCool™?

Flux Pods

SkyCool™ Flux Pods are advanced thermal management systems designed to optimize cooling in various applications. By utilizing innovative materials and AI-driven algorithms, these pods maintain ideal temperature conditions while minimizing energy consumption. They are engineered to adapt dynamically to environmental changes, ensuring consistent performance even under extreme conditions.

Stay Alert. Protect Yourself from Fraud.

Reliance Industries Limited is committed to your safety and security. We have been made aware of fraudulent activities and scams falsely claiming to represent our company.

We will never request your personal banking details, passwords, or payments via unsolicited calls, emails, or messages.

Common Types of Fraud

  • Fake job offers asking for money or personal details.
  • Investment scams promising unrealistic returns.
  • Phishing emails pretending to be from Reliance.
  • Fake websites or social media profiles using our name/logo.

How to Stay Safe

  • Verify any communication by contacting our official channels.
  • Do not click on suspicious links or open unknown attachments.
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately to our fraud department.
